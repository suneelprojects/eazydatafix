# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and welcoming.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.
- Helping newcomers learn and contribute.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or insulting comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Trolling or disruptive behavior.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional environment.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub discussions, issues, pull requests, documentation, and community interactions.

---

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainer.
